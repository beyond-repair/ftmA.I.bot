Trading Techniques List:

1. Fundamental Analysis (FA)

Fundamental analysis is a method of evaluating securities by attempting to measure its intrinsic value by examining related economic, financial and other qualitative and quantitative factors.

Implementation: The trading bot could utilize economic and financial data and other related factors to determine current market trends. 

2. Technical Analysis (TA)

Technical analysis involves evaluating securities solely on the basis of past prices. By charting the stock's price over a period of time and looking for patterns, traders can start to predict future price movements.

Implementation: Given the availability of historical price data, the trading bot can utilize technical indicators to predict price movements and execute trades accordingly.
